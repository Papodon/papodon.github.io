About

Welcome to Belanos, a website inspired by the solarpunk movement and dedicated to creating a sustainable future that's deeply interconnected with nature and community.

At Belanos, we believe that technology can play an integral role in building a better world. We envision a future where technology is not just a tool for profit and efficiency, but a means of creating a more harmonious relationship between humans and the natural world.

As part of the indieweb, Belanos is committed to decentralization and empowering individuals to own and control their online presence. We believe that the internet should be a place for community and collaboration, not just a marketplace for big tech companies.

At Belanos, we are optimistic about the future. We believe that by coming together and harnessing the power of technology, we can build a world that's sustainable, just, and equitable for all.
